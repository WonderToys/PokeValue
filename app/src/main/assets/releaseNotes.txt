<strong><big>Usage</big></strong><br />
<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Click "Open Tray"<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Tap the 3 dots to toggle the tray expanded and collapsed<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Tap the "E" icon to open the Calculate window<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Long press the tray (or Calculate window) to move it<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;- Tap the "X" icon to close the tray<br />
